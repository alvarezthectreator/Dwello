Common Questions

Valuation FAQs

How accurate is the instant estimate?
Our algorithm is typically within 5–8% of actual transacted prices, based on back-testing against 12 months of URA data. For the highest accuracy, we recommend pairing the instant estimate with a free in-person appraisal from a Dwelro agent.
Is there any cost or obligation?
Absolutely none. The instant estimate and full agent appraisal are completely free. We don't charge homeowners for valuations — our business model is commission-based only if you choose to list with us.
What data sources do you use?
We use real-time data from the Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA), HDB Resale Portal, and our own proprietary database of off-market transactions. This is supplemented by local agent intelligence from our network of 120+ CEA-certified advisors.
How soon will an agent contact me?
Our agents typically reach out within 2 hours during business hours (9am–7pm), and by 9am the following morning for evening submissions. You can also choose to book directly via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.
Does my valuation expire?
Market conditions change, so we recommend refreshing your estimate every 3–6 months if you are actively monitoring your property's value. You can re-run the instant estimate anytime at no cost.
I'm not selling — can I still get a valuation?
Of course. Many of our clients request valuations for estate planning, refinancing, divorce proceedings, or simply out of curiosity. There is no requirement to sell, and no pressure from our team to do so.

Prefer a human expert?

Skip the form and speak directly with one of our senior valuers. We'll arrange a home visit at a time that suits you — free of charge, no strings attached.
